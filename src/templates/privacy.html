Privacy Policy

The purpose of the XKCDBot service is to provide Slack users with a quick and easy way to include relevant XKCD comics in their conversations.

XKCDBot is an automated service which provides limited automatic filtering only. If you have any questions or comments, please contact us directly at: support@xkcdbot.com
